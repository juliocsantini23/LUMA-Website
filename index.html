Lumachain (LUMA) is more than just a token — it is a project built on trust, transparency, and integrity.

In a market full of speculation, we stand for clarity, fairness, and sustainable growth.

Our Vision is Simple

🔐 Build Secure Digital Assets

Build a secure digital asset supported by verified smart contracts with complete transparency.

📈 Guarantee Long-term Stability

Guarantee long-term stability with a gradual liquidity strategy (up to 70% released to the market).

👥 Create Community-Driven Ecosystem

Create a community-driven ecosystem, where every step is visible on-chain and guided by integrity.

Our Philosophy

Lumachain is not designed for hype.

How to Buy LUMA

Follow these simple steps to purchase LumaChain tokens safely and securely.

1

Get a Wallet

Download and configure a compatible wallet for the Polygon Network:

👻
Phantom
🦊
MetaMask

⚙️ Make sure to configure it for the Polygon Network

2

Get USDC

Purchase USDC from a trusted exchange and send it to your wallet:

🟡
Binance
🔵
Coinbase

📤 Send the USDC to your wallet using the Polygon network

3

Connect to SushiSwap

Access SushiSwap and connect your wallet:

🍣
Go to SushiSwap

⚠️ IMPORTANT: Make sure you are on the Polygon network!

On SushiSwap, verify that the selected network is "Polygon" in the top right corner.

4

Add LUMA Token

Add the LUMA token to your swap interface using our contract address:

0x453453096e669a8a.faA55bd64b

🔍 Always verify the contract address on PolygonScan before trading

5

Make the Swap

Configure your swap and complete the transaction:

💱
Select USDC as the "From" token
🎯
Select LUMA as the "To" token
💰
Enter the amount you want to swap
⚡
Review the transaction and confirm

🛡️ Always double-check the contract address and transaction details before confirming

✅
